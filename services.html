Our Services

At Atlantic Gold Exchange, we offer a wide range of services to meet all your buying and selling needs, including:

Jewelry Appraisal and Buying

Our expert appraisers will evaluate your gold, silver, platinum, and diamond jewelry and offer you top dollar for your items.

Coin Appraisal and Buying

We specialize in buying and selling rare and valuable coins, including gold, silver, and platinum coins from around the world

Estate Jewelry and Coin Buying

We understand that selling estate jewelry and coins can be an emotional and stressful experience. Our team is committed to providing compassionate and professional service to help you navigate this process with ease.

Investment in Precious Metals

Whether you're looking to invest in gold, silver, or platinum, we can provide you with expert guidance and advice to help you make informed decisions.
