Refinery

At Atlantic Precious Metals Refinery (located in Mars, PA), we use the latest technology and equipment to refine gold, silver, platinum, and other precious metals into high-quality products. For over 30 years, our experienced team of professionals has ensured that every batch of metal is refined to the highest standard, so you can be confident that the products you receive from us are of the best quality.

By using our refining services, you can benefit from:

Competitive Pricing

We offer some of the most competitive pricing in the industry, so you can increase your profits and remain competitive.

High-Quality Products

Our refined metals are of the highest quality, so you can be confident that you are providing your customers with the best products.

Timely Delivery

We understand that time is of the essence in your business, so we ensure that your products are delivered to you in a timely manner.
