About
Atlantic

Welcome to Atlantic Gold Exchange, your trusted source for buying and selling jewelry and coins in the Greater Pittsburgh area. We are a family-owned and operated business with over 25 years of experience in the industry, and our team of expert appraisers provides the highest level of customer service and satisfaction.

More About Us

We Buy & Appraise the following:

  • Gold, Silver, Platinum, and Palladium Coins
  • Gold, Silver, Sterling Silver, and Platinum Jewelry
  • Gold, Silver, Platinum, and Palladium Bullion
  • Sterling Silver Items (Candle sticks, Flatware, Frames, etc)

Why Atlantic is Unique

Atlantic Gold Exchange is owned and operated by Atlantic Precious Metals Refinery of Mars, PA. There is no middleman or broker in our buying and appraisal process. What you’ll receive is the best price direct from the ultimate buyer – no extra markup and no extra fees. Bring it all in and let Atlantic sort it out!
